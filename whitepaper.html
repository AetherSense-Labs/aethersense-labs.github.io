1. Vision

AetherSense represents the intersection of decentralized edge computing and on-chain intelligence. Built natively on TON, we provide tools for the next generation of users who demand personalized, private, and high-performance AI.

3. Tokenomics ($ATHS)

The $ATHS token is the lifeblood of the ecosystem, designed as a standard utility Jetton with a fixed supply.

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 $ATHS
  • Contract: Standard Jetton (Zero Tax, Non-mintable)
  • Use Cases: Payment for generation of personalized personas, access to premium API tiers, and ecosystem governance.
